Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for actual daily use: reading, studying, tracking progress, and staying consistent.

Reading

Pick up right where you left off

Olive Branch remembers your place down to the verse. The Home screen shows exactly where you are with one tap to jump back in. No hunting through chapter lists.

  • Continue Reading card always visible on Home
  • In Progress badge on books you've started
  • Light, dark, and sepia reading themes
  • Adjustable font size with live preview
  • Toggle verse numbers on or off
  • Continuous scroll through chapters
Study Plans

A reading plan that fits how you actually live

Choose a preset or build a completely custom plan. Pick the books you want, set your own deadline, and the app calculates your daily pace automatically.

  • Full Bible in 1 Year, Full Bible in 2 Years
  • New Testament in 90 or 30 Days
  • Prophets or Psalms in 30 Days
  • Custom: any books, any deadline
  • Daily progress bar and chapters-to-go counter
  • Streak tracking across your whole reading history
Search

Find any verse in seconds

Full-text search across the entire Bible. All 1,200+ chapters indexed locally. No network request, no delay. Just results.

  • Search across all 66+ books simultaneously
  • Results show book, chapter, and full verse text
  • Tap any result to jump directly to it
  • Works completely offline

The 2001 Translation

A translation that doesn't soften the original.

Most English Bibles carry centuries of tradition, and centuries of translators making choices that obscure what the original text actually says. The 2001 Translation starts fresh from the Greek and Hebrew manuscripts, prioritizing accuracy over familiarity.

Olive Branch is built specifically for this translation, preserving every detail that makes it distinct.
